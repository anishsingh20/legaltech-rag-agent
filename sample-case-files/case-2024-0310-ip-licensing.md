# Case File 2024-0310: Lumen Bio IP Licensing Dispute

**Matter ID:** 2024-0310  
**Client:** Lumen Bio Therapeutics  
**Counterparty:** Helix Research Partners  
**Jurisdiction:** SDNY  
**Filed:** 2024-05-03  
**Status:** Motion to dismiss pending

## Summary

Lumen Bio seeks declaratory judgment that its CRISPR delivery method does not infringe Helix Patent US-10,998,221 after Helix sent a cease-and-desist letter on 2024-04-11.

## Patent at Issue

- **Patent:** US-10,998,221
- **Title:** Lipid nanoparticle formulations for guide RNA delivery
- **Priority date:** 2017-06-14

## Lumen Position

- Lumen uses a distinct PEGylation ratio (8:1 vs Helix claimed 4:1).
- Prior art reference WO2018/044112 anticipates claims 1-4.
- No licensing agreement exists between parties.

## Helix Position (from C&D letter)

- Lumen Phase 2 trial protocol mirrors claim 7 dependent elements.
- Demands 2.5% net sales royalty or litigation.
