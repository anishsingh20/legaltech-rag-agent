# Case File 2024-0142: Meridian Analytics NDA Breach

**Matter ID:** 2024-0142  
**Client:** Northwind Logistics LLC  
**Opposing Party:** Meridian Analytics Inc.  
**Jurisdiction:** Delaware Chancery Court  
**Filed:** 2024-03-18  
**Status:** Discovery

## Summary

Northwind Logistics alleges Meridian Analytics disclosed confidential pricing models and customer pipeline data to a competitor after signing a mutual NDA on 2023-11-02.

## Key Facts

- NDA executed on 2023-11-02 with a 24-month confidentiality term.
- Joint evaluation period ran from 2023-11-15 through 2024-01-30.
- On 2024-02-14, Northwind learned Meridian shared a slide deck containing Northwind unit economics with Apex Data Systems.
- The slide deck filename was `Northwind_Pricing_v3_confidential.pptx`.
- Meridian employee Sarah Chen sent the file via personal Gmail on 2024-02-09.

## Damages Claimed

- Lost enterprise contract with Harbor Freight Group: $1.2M annual value.
- Remediation and audit costs: $84,000.
- Injunctive relief requested to stop further disclosure.
